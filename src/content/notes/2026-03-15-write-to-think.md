Writing about code forces clarity. If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it deeply enough.

This notebook exists because writing is thinking. Every entry here started as confusion and ended as understanding. The act of putting thoughts into words is the act of learning itself.

Writing is not a record of what you know. It's a process of figuring out what you know.

That's why this notebook will never stop growing.
