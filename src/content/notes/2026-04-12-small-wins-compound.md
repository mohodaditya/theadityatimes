One refactored function today. A cleaner codebase by month end. Consistency beats intensity every time.

I stopped doing weekend-long refactoring sprints. Instead, I improve one small thing every day. The codebase has never been cleaner.

The same principle applies to learning. Reading one article a day compounds faster than binge-reading on weekends.

Small, consistent effort is invisible day-to-day but transformative over months.
