Redesigning my portfolio as a newspaper forced me to think about hierarchy, flow, and editorial storytelling.

Every section became a newspaper column. Every project became a headline. The constraint of print layout pushed me to prioritize what truly matters.

Sometimes the most creative solutions come from the most unusual constraints. A portfolio doesn't have to look like every other portfolio.
