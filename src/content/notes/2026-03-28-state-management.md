Most apps don't need Redux. Context and useReducer handle 90% of real-world state elegantly.

I removed Redux from a mid-sized project and replaced it with three contexts. The bundle shrunk by 12KB. The code became readable. The team became happier.

Reach for the simplest tool that solves the problem. Complexity should be earned, not assumed.

The best architecture is the one your team can understand without a wiki.
