Building DeshKaRojgar taught me that fewer features with better execution always wins over feature bloat.

Every feature I removed made the product better. Every shortcut I eliminated made the user journey clearer. Simplicity isn't about doing less — it's about doing the right things exceptionally well.

The hardest part of building a product isn't adding features. It's deciding which ones to remove.
